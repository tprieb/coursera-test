Ou's Bakery

Macarons

Our macarons come in a variety of flavors and are made with French meringue to create a glossy, smooth finish. They are perfect for parties, party favors, or even to satisfy that sweet tooth craving. Macarons are gluten free and can be made dairy-free.

Eclairs

Eclairs are oblong pastries made with choux dough and filled with cream. They are crispy and on the outside and chewy on the inside. Our eclairs are made either with vanilla filling or chocolate filling. Try one of each and decide which way you prefer!

Cupcakes

These individual-sized cakes come with their own frosting piled high on top! Try our favorite flavor or let us help you customize your own. The combinations are endless!
